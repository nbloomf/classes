\item Using polynomial long division, find the quotient and remainder when \[ a(x) = x^5 - 4x^4 + 14x^2 - 17x + 6 \] is divided by \[ b(x) = x^3 - 2x^2 - 5x + 6. \] \vspace{6cm}
